This site started on `nickstambaugh.dev` because that's what it was, a development environment that happened to be public. Soon we will be upgrading to `.com`, which is a callout to moving the site out of development and into a more professional space where I can share art.

## What v1 Actually Means

I'm calling it v1 because I stopped moving the goalposts. 

The portfolio reflects work I've done. The blog has real posts. The [music page](/music) is starting to have music on it.

## What's Coming Next

I'm opening up my art for sale soon. Music first, and possibly a photography book down the road. 

If you've listened to anything on the [music page](/music) and want to license it or collaborate, reach out at [nickstambaugh@proton.me](mailto:nickstambaugh@proton.me).

I'm also pulling back from freelance work. That time is going toward making music instead. It's a pivot I kept putting off due to wanting more. More in my career. More in my bank account.

It's time!
