## How to Hibernate Your LinkedIn Account

#### Introduction

LinkedIn is a piece of hot garbage and full of vanity, but we need it from time to time when looking for a job. 

Luckily, LinkedIn offers a "Hibernation" feature that allows you to step away while keeping your profile, connections, and content intact for a future return.

This guide covers what happens when you hibernate your account and how to execute the process.

---

#### What Happens During Hibernation?

When you choose to hibernate your account, LinkedIn effectively hides your presence from the platform.

* **Profile Visibility:** Your profile becomes invisible to everyone, including your connections.
* **Search:** You will no longer appear in search results, including professional tools like LinkedIn Recruiter.
* **Engagement:** Your previous posts, comments, and recommendations will remain, but your name will be replaced by **"A LinkedIn member."** If you had a profile photo, it will be replaced by a generic icon.
* **Messages:** Existing message threads will persist, but you will be identified as "A LinkedIn member."

> **Important Note:** You must wait at least **24 hours** after hibernating your account before you can attempt to reactivate it.

---

# How to Hibernate Your Account

Before proceeding, ensure you have addressed any outstanding administrative tasks (such as paying balances or reassigning group admin roles).

1.  **Navigate to Settings:** Click your profile photo at the top of your LinkedIn homepage and select **Settings & Privacy**.
2.  **Access Management:** Select **Account management** from the menu.
3.  **Hibernate:** Click on **Hibernate account**.
4.  **Reasoning:** Select the option that best describes why you are choosing to hibernate.
5.  **Finalize:** Enter your password and click the **Hibernate account** button.

*Note: If you have a Premium subscription, it will be canceled immediately. If you pay through Apple, ensure you also cancel the subscription within your Apple settings to prevent future billing.*

---

#### Reactivating Your Account

> Pro tip: Only reactivate your account once you are job searching.

When you are ready to return to the platform:

1.  **Log In:** Wait at least 24 hours after the initial hibernation.
2.  **Credentials:** Sign in using your standard email and password.
3.  **Restoration:** Your profile will be restored.

**Expect a slight delay:** Upon reactivation, you might notice temporary discrepancies in your connection or follower counts. This is normal and typically resolves itself within 48 hours.
